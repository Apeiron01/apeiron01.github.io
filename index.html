Data Safety

Apps are designed with data minimization in mind. Personally identifiable information is not collected unless a feature explicitly requires it. If advertising or analytics SDKs are used (e.g., Google AdMob), they may process limited non-personal signals (such as device identifiers or coarse region) under their own policies and controls.

  • Prefer offline/local processing for core features when possible.
  • No unnecessary permissions (contacts, SMS, precise location) by default.
  • Users can manage ad personalization via device/Google settings where applicable.
